About Me

Learn more about my academic journey and research interests

I am a postdoctoral researcher at DTU Wind and Energy Systems (Denmark), working with Prof. Spyros Chatzivasileiadis. I obtained my Ph.D in Mathematical Engineering from Universidad Carlos III de Madrid, where I was supervised by Prof. Carlos Ruiz Mora. My doctoral research focused on machine learning and surrogate modeling for optimization.

My research interests include machine learning, probabilistic forecasting, data-driven optimization, and surrogate modeling, with a particular focus on applications in sustainable energy systems. Further information about my research activities and achievements is available in my list of publications and academic Curriculum Vitae.
